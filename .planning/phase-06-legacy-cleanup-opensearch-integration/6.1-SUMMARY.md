## Decisions Made

- The migration intentionally treats `member` and `superadmin` as known transitional values so it can rewrite them while still failing fast on truly unknown data.
- The rollback path stays a no-op because the real rollback lever is the Phase 3 feature flag, not restoring stale role aliases into `users.role`.
