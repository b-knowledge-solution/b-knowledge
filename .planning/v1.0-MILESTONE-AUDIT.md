## Critical Gaps

### 1. Orphaned Document Viewer Components (DOCM-02)
`CitationDocDrawer` and `SearchResultDocDialog` were created in Phase 3 Plan 03 but never imported by any chat or search component. Users cannot preview source documents from citations or search results.

**Fix:** Wire CitationDocDrawer into ChatPage citation click handler. Wire SearchResultDocDialog into SearchResultCard click handler.

### 2. Deep Research Progress Not Displayed (RETR-04, RETR-06)
`useChatStream` correctly parses `deepResearchEvents` from SSE, but ChatPage never accesses or renders them. Users see no progress during Deep Research.

**Fix:** Add DeepResearchProgress component to ChatPage that renders `stream.deepResearchEvents` (subquery status, budget warnings).

### 3. ABAC Field Filters Not Applied in Cross-Dataset Search (RETR-07)
`buildOpenSearchAbacFilters` is imported in `chat-conversation.service.ts` but never called. `userAbacFilters` is always an empty array. Per-dataset ABAC policy conditions (e.g., "only documents where department=finance") are silently ignored.

**Fix:** Call `buildOpenSearchAbacFilters(tenantId, userPolicies)` and pass result to `searchMultipleDatasets`.
